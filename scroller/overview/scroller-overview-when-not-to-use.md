Do not use **Scroller** when scrolling is not present or not expected.

- Do not place the Scroller on containers where overflow is hidden or content is fully visible without scrolling — a visible scrollbar on a non-scrolling surface creates a false affordance
- Do not use the Scroller as a progress indicator — use **Progress Bar** for communicating task or load progress
- Do not use the Scroller in place of **Pagination** when content is intentionally paginated rather than continuously scrolled
